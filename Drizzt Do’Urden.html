Drizzt   Do`Urden
Or the saga of the dark elf

The series of novels about Dirt Do'Urden is a book series by Robert Salvatore, the action of which takes place in the fictional universe Forgotten Realms.

The genre of the cycle is most often defined as game or heroic fantasy. The main character of the cycle is a drow scout named Dzirt Do'Urden.

The cycle does not have a common official name, but the following names are widely used: "Dzyrt Saga", "Dzyrt Do'Urden Cycle", "Dark Elf Saga", "Dzyrt Do'Urden" and so on.

Sometimes the name of the first trilogy "Dark Elf" is used to denote the entire cycle.

At the moment, 40 books have been written, the last of which was published in August 2023.

In addition to novels, the cycle also includes several short stories: "Dark Mirror", "Entry Fee", "Friends Meet in Trouble" and a short prequel "Guenvivar".
