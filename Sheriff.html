Sheriff

The novel "Sheriff" is a book in the popular genre of LitRPG today.

With a very important difference from analogues: there is irony over the genre itself, there is an element of parody of works in this genre.

The main character of the novel is Jonathan Lawrence, whose spaceship broke down and got stuck on an asteroid.

Will the rescuers wait a long time, and will they arrive at all? Jonathan concludes a contract with the company that created the most popular online game and immerses himself in virtuality.

But, having found himself in the game world and becoming the sheriff of the city, the hero does not change his principles.

Even in the game, even "accidentally", he refuses to "die", despite the guaranteed immortality of his character, he does not engage in his "pumping" and collection of artifacts at all, but continues to behave in the same way as in real life.

At the same time, he quickly achieves what others take years to achieve. It is not surprising that over time, Jonathan attracts the close attention of the entire virtual world.

How will this story end and is it possible to survive in the game while remaining a person with firm moral values and an open soul?
