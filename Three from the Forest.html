Three from the Forest

Three from the Forest" is a series of books by Yuri Nikitin about the adventures of three neuroses, exiled from their tribe, living in the deep forest, to the big world.

The first published book of the series ("Three from the Forest") was also the first book written in the genre of the heroic direction of Russian fantasy.

The cycle illustrates the development of humanity from "ancient times" to the present day, raising questions of predestination, the search for "truth for all", building a world based on justice, the development of world religions and the role of technological progress in society.

The author offers the concept of changing the perception of the world by mankind as it matures - yes, at first it is a flat world resting on a turtle, by the end of the cycle - peace in the modern scientific representation.
